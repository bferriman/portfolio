About Me

I am an aspiring web developer sharpening my skills at the University of Richmond Coding Boot Camp. I am building on the knowledge I gained earning my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Denver (class of 2004).

I began my career in the financial services industry, working at Charles Schwab & Co. from 2004 to 2010. As a licensed broker, I worked extensively with financial management and trading web sites and desktop applications. From 2010 to 2013, I contributed in many roles at Offbeat Entertainment - a startup music-focused video game development studio. I led the team that developed content for the Rock Band Network and was the primary content designer for the Facebook game Rockstar Rising.

After spending the past few years at home with my young son, I am excited to be updating my coding skills! In my free time, I enjoy video games, board games, Magic: The Gathering, and outdoor activities.
